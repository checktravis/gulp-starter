Float Center Galleries

You might not need the power of self correcting galleries we saw in the last template. But you might still want to center the items within Susy for a gallery layout.

It is possible to do so quickly and easily by setting display: inline-block to each gallery__item and text-align: center to .gallery.

Note: This is a hacky fix. You will need nudge each gallery item to the left by 4px because there are some unremoveable white space between inline-block items.
