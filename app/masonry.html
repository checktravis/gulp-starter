Masonry With Susy

You can use Masonry to organize your gallery items if they are of unequal height. This template explains how to use Masonry with Susy.

You'll need need 4 different elements when setting up a Masonry grid.

  1. .masonry – The Masonry grid container
  2. .masonry__column – A div to determine the Masonry column size
  3. .masonry__gutter – A div to determine the Masonry gutter size
  4. .masonry__item – The gallery item

Have a look at masonry.scss and masonry.js to view the source code.
